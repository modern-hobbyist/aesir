This KiCad template for the STM32G474RET6 keyboard is your go-to for designing custom mechanical keyboards. It’s got a well-organized layout for key switches and components, making it easy to tweak and modify to fit your needs. You’ll find everything you need to start building, with clear layers and footprints to guide you through the process. Check out the STM32G474RET6 at Digi-Key for more details.
