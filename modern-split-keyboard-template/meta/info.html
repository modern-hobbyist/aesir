This split keyboard template for the STM32F072CBT6 MCU is perfect for building an ergonomic, two-piece keyboard. Each half has its own STM32F072CBT6, allowing for easy customization and modular design. The layout ensures smooth alignment between the halves, and you can easily adapt it to your preferred key configuration. Explore the STM32F072CBT6 at Digi-Key for more info.
